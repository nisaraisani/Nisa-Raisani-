Migraine: Evidence Based Management

Clinical Review Article

By Dr. Nisa Raisani, MD – Consultant Neurologist
Published: January 2025 · 6 min read

Abstract

An overview of evidence-based diagnosis and management of migraine headaches. Includes clinical guidelines and therapeutic options.

Keywords: Migraine, Neurology, Headache Treatment

Introduction

Migraine is a chronic neurological disorder characterized by recurrent headaches, often associated with nausea, photophobia, and phonophobia.

Clinical Diagnosis

Diagnosis is primarily clinical, based on patient history and symptom patterns. International guidelines assist in accurate classification.

Treatment Strategies

Management includes acute treatment, preventive therapy, and lifestyle modification based on patient-specific factors.

Conclusion

Evidence-based migraine management improves patient outcomes and quality of life.
