About

Professional and Academic Profile

Profile

Dr. Nisa Raisani is a neurologist and PhD researcher with a strong academic and clinical orientation toward brain and mental health sciences. Her work integrates patient-centered neurological care with rigorous scientific research. She is actively involved in global research collaborations, academic writing, and evidence-based practice, with the aim of improving neurological and mental health outcomes at both clinical and population levels.

She maintains active academic profiles and contributes to international research platforms, reflecting a commitment to transparent, reproducible, and impactful medical research.

Expertise

Her professional expertise lies in clinical neurology with particular experience in the diagnosis and management of headache disorders, epilepsy, movement disorders, and conditions affecting cognition and behavior. She adopts a patient-centered and evidence-based approach to neurological care.

Her work also extends into mental and cognitive health, where she focuses on the interface between neurology and psychiatry, addressing cognitive decline, neuropsychiatric symptoms, and mental health conditions from a neuroscientific perspective.

In addition to clinical practice, she is actively involved in clinical and academic research. Her experience includes literature synthesis, study design, data interpretation, and preparation of scholarly manuscripts for peer-reviewed journals. She regularly engages in academic writing and international research collaboration, contributing to multidisciplinary teams and global research networks.
