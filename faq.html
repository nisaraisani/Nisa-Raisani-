Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What conditions do you treat?

I specialize in neurological conditions including migraine, epilepsy, Parkinson’s disease, memory disorders, and other nervous system conditions.

❓ Do you provide online consultations?

Online consultations may be offered for selected cases. Appointment requests are reviewed before confirmation.

❓ Is medical advice provided through email?

No. Email communication is limited to appointment coordination only. Medical advice is provided strictly during consultations.

❓ How can I request an appointment?

You can submit an appointment request through the Appointment page. Confirmation is sent after review.

❓ Is my information kept confidential?

Yes. All submitted information is handled confidentially and used only for appointment-related purposes.

❓ Are emergency cases handled here?

No. This platform does not provide emergency services. In case of an emergency, please contact local emergency services immediately.
