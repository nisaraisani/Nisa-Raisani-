Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What conditions do you treat?

I provide consultation and management for neurological conditions including migraine and headache disorders, epilepsy, Parkinson’s disease, memory and cognitive disorders, anxiety related neurological symptoms, and general neurological complaints.

Do you provide online consultations?

Yes. Online consultations are available for selected cases. These are intended for medical guidance, follow-up care, and second opinions. Emergency conditions require immediate in-person medical attention.

Is this consultation a replacement for emergency care?

No. Online or outpatient consultations do not replace emergency services. If you experience sudden weakness, loss of consciousness, seizures, severe headache, or vision loss, seek emergency medical care immediately.

How do I book an appointment?

Appointments can be requested through the Appointment page. After submission, you will receive a confirmation email once your request has been reviewed.

Will my personal information remain confidential?

Yes. All patient information is handled with strict confidentiality and is used only for clinical communication purposes. No data is shared with third parties.

Can I get medical advice through email or WhatsApp?

Medical advice is not provided via email or WhatsApp. These channels are used only for appointment coordination and general information.

Do you prescribe medications online?

Medication decisions are made on a case by case basis. Prescriptions may require proper clinical evaluation and may not be possible in all situations.

What languages are consultations available in?

Consultations are available in English and Urdu.
