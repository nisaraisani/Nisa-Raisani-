About Dr. Nisa Raisani

Dr. Nisa Raisani is a consultant neurologist and academic researcher with a strong focus on evidence-based neurological care, clinical excellence, and medical education. Her professional work combines patient-centered clinical practice with international research contributions.

She has clinical expertise in the management of migraine and headache disorders, epilepsy, Parkinson’s disease, and cognitive or memory-related conditions. Dr. Raisani emphasizes ethical medical practice, patient privacy, and informed decision-making.

In addition to clinical care, Dr. Raisani actively contributes to global medical research. Her scholarly work is indexed across international academic platforms, supporting transparent and open-access medical knowledge.
