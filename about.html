About Dr. Nisa Raisani

Expert neurologist and PhD researcher in mental health, integrating patient-centered care with cutting-edge research and international collaborations.

Professional Profile

Dr. Nisa Raisani is a board-certified neurologist with extensive experience in clinical neurology, cognitive health, and mental health research. She combines hands-on patient care with rigorous academic research to advance neuroscience globally.

Clinical & Research Expertise

Neurological Disorders: Headache & migraine, epilepsy, movement disorders, stroke, neurodegenerative conditions.

Mental Health: Cognitive evaluation, dementia, neuropsychiatric disorders, anxiety & depression.

Research Focus: Translational neuroscience, clinical outcomes, public health impact, innovative therapies.

Advanced Diagnostics: EEG, neuroimaging interpretation, cognitive assessment tools.

Therapeutic Interventions: Pharmacological, neurorehabilitation, lifestyle & preventive strategies.

Global Collaborations: Publications, conferences, and partnerships with international research institutes.

Education & Achievements

MBBS, FCPS (Neurology), PhD in Mental Health & Neuroscience. Multiple awards for research excellence, international presentations, and clinical contributions to neurology.
