Migraine Treatment

Migraine is a neurological condition that causes recurring headaches and other symptoms. Dr Nisa Raisani provides evidence-based migraine diagnosis and personalized treatment plans.

What is Migraine?

Migraine is more than a headache. It is a neurological disorder that affects quality of life and requires professional medical care.

Symptoms of Migraine

  • Throbbing or pulsating headache
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Sensitivity to light and sound
  • Visual aura or blurred vision
  • Fatigue and dizziness

Causes and Triggers

Common migraine triggers include stress, lack of sleep, hormonal changes, dehydration, and certain foods.

Migraine Diagnosis

Diagnosis is based on clinical history and neurological examination. Additional tests may be required in some cases.

Treatment Options

  • Preventive migraine medications
  • Acute pain relief therapies
  • Lifestyle and trigger management
  • Stress control and sleep regulation

When to Consult a Neurologist?

If migraine attacks are frequent or severe, early consultation can help prevent complications and improve long-term outcomes.
