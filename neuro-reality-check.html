Neuro Reality Check

Neuro Reality Check is a short, neuroscience-inspired self-awareness experience. It helps you reflect on attention, reaction awareness, and mental load. This is not a medical test or diagnosis.

Purpose

To encourage early awareness of cognitive strain before symptoms are ignored.

What It Reflects

Momentary focus, reaction awareness, and cognitive consistency — not disease.

Time Required

Less than one minute. No login. No data storage.

60-Second Reaction Awareness Check

Click Start. When the box turns green, tap it as quickly as you can. This interaction reflects attention and reaction awareness at this moment.

Disclaimer: This awareness tool does not diagnose neurological conditions and does not replace professional medical consultation.
