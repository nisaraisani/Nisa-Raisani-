Neuro Reality Check

Neuro Reality Check is a short, neuroscience-inspired self-awareness experience. It helps you reflect on attention, reaction awareness, and mental load. This is not a medical test or diagnosis.

Purpose

To encourage early awareness of cognitive strain before symptoms are ignored.

What It Reflects

Focus consistency and reaction awareness at this moment — not disease.

Time Required

Less than one minute. No login. No data storage.

Your 60-Second Reality Check

Click Start. When the box turns green, tap it as quickly as you can. This simple interaction reflects attention and reaction awareness.
