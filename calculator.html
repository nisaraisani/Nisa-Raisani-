BMI (Body Mass Index)

What is BMI?
BMI estimates body fat using height and weight.

WHO Categories:
• Underweight: < 18.5
• Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
• Overweight: 25 – 29.9
• Obese: ≥ 30

BSA (Body Surface Area)

What is BSA?
BSA represents total body surface area and is widely used for drug dosing, chemotherapy and pediatrics.

Average BSA:
• Adult men (14+): ~ 1.9 m²
• Adult women: ~ 1.6 m²
