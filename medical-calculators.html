Medical Calculators

Our evidence based calculators are designed for healthcare professionals to assist in accurate clinical decision-making. Each tool provides calculated values, reference ranges, risk classification (normal, caution, high-risk), and guidance.

Applications include BMI, BSA, pediatric & adult dosing, fluid & infusion calculations, renal function (Creatinine Clearance, eGFR), pregnancy assessments, and insulin dosing.

Note: These tools are for educational purposes only and do not replace professional medical judgment.
