How Online Consultation Works

Patients may request an online neurological consultation by submitting the appointment form available on this website. Each request is carefully reviewed. If the consultation is appropriate, confirmation and payment instructions are sent via email.

Payment Method

Payments for online consultations are accepted via Skrill (Moneybookers). Payment is requested only after your consultation request has been reviewed and approved. Detailed payment instructions will be provided by email.

Important Notice

Online consultations are provided for advisory and educational purposes only. They do not replace in-person medical evaluation and are not suitable for emergency conditions. In case of a medical emergency, please seek immediate local medical care.
